<objective>
Eliminate silent InMemory fallbacks in production and enforce LOGS_BUCKET_NAME as a required environment variable.

Purpose: In production with multiple Cloud Run replicas, InMemorySessionService and InMemoryArtifactService cause data loss (sessions vanish on replica restart, artifacts are not persisted). The app must fail fast at startup rather than silently degrading, forcing operators to fix infrastructure before serving traffic.

Output: Modified startup guards in fast_api_app.py, LOGS_BUCKET_NAME added to production validation, and unit tests proving the fail-fast behavior.
</objective>
